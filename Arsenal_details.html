Arsenal is Armor penetraction based Multiplayer first person shooter game.

Arsenal has unique penetration system inspired by world of tanks and war thunder. Player can choose light armored, fast phase play style or take heavy armor and become tanker. Heavy armor can withstand heavy firepower but it cost a lot so you always take care of exchange ratio. We provide Team Deathmatch 5 : 5 PVP Game with AI which inspired by titanfall game. AI grunts are way easy target but it rewards less points to player. First team reaches the goal will win.
